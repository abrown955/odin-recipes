Baked Dijon Salmon

Description

This mustard-crusted salmon is a wonderful way to prepare fresh salmon fillets in the oven. Be sure to make extra â€” your family will be begging for more!

Ingredients

  • butter
  • dijon mustard
  • honey
  • bread crumbs
  • pecans
  • parsley
  • salmon
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Mix together bread crumbs, pecans, and parsley in another bowl
  3. Brush each salmon fillet lightly with honey mustard mix
  4. Sprinkle the tops of fillets with bread crumb mixture
  5. Bake salmon in the preheated oven until it flakes easily with a fork, 12-15 minutes
